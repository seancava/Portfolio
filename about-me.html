About Sean Cavanaugh

I was born in a small town in Northern Ontario. My parents didn't have much but we made do and were happy. When my parents finished school my father landed his first job with the Texaco Corporation. We moved South and continued on as a happy family. When I was 5 years old I started a long career in public school. Mostly the days were drudgery with occassional relief of socializing with my peers. Later when I entered highschool I was able to taylor my studies to suit my intrests. Ater highschool I persued post secondary education at various schools studying various forms of visual art and design. After graduating with a diploma I set out to begin a career in graphic design. I worked here and there for some years before starting my own graphic design freelancing business.
